Minestrone

Description

This tasty minestrone soup is full of nutritious vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, halved lengthwise and sliced thinly
  • 3 celery ribs, quartered lengthwise and sliced
  • 28oz diced fire roasted tomatoes
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • 14 oz canned cannellini beans
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1 cup macaroni
  • Salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes to taste
  • Baguette for serving

Steps

  1. Heat oil on stove. Cook onion, carrot, garlic, and celery in the oil for 5 minutes or until lightly browned. Stir in tomatoes.
  2. Stir in broth and spices. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Add pasta and cook until tender, about 12 minutes. Serve warm with toasted bread.
