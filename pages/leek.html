Leek and Potato Stew

Description

This leek and potato stew is perfect for warming up on a cold winter night.

Ingredients

  • 6 large yukon gold potatoes, about 3 pounds, cut into 1/2" chunks
  • 2 leeks, cleaned and sliced thinly
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 slices bacon
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 1 cup half and half or whole milk
  • 4oz sharp cheddar cheese
  • Salt, pepper, and paprika to taste
  • Serve with sourdough

Steps

  1. Cook bacon on the stove in the soup pot until crisp. Remove bacon and let dry on a bed of paper towels.
  2. Cook leeks and onion in the leftover bacon grease until tender, about 5 minutes
  3. Stir potatoes and chicken broth into mixture. Simmer for 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
  4. Add half and half or milk to potato soup and simmer until thickened, about 15 minutes.
  5. Stir in cheese and seasonings until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
  6. Serve warm with sourdough. Soup will save well for several days.
