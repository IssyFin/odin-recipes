White Chicken Chili

Description

This white chicken chili comes together in a pressure cooker in a flash for a hot, easy weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken breast
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 31 oz canned great northern beans
  • 1 can corn
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • Sharp cheddar cheese, shredded, for serving
  • Tortilla chips, for serving

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except sour cream, milk, cheese, and tortilla chips in pressure cooker. Cook on high for 20 minutes.
  2. Quick release steam and shred chicken.
  3. Whisk in sour cream and milk.
  4. Serve warm with cheese and tortilla chips.
